About

Thank you for helping me gather the data I need to complete my master's thesis! You'll find detailed instructions included with your download.

In the context of Western music, we recognize that music usually adheres to some form of hierarchical structure. In a classical piece this might be the A-B-A sontata-allegro form. In pop music this could be the Verse-Chorus-Verse-Chorus-Bridge-Chorus-Chorus form. Hymn melodies and jazz solos can contain repeated motives and phrases, and we can even see observe nested patterns occurring in simple lullabys:

Our goal is to evalute existing algorithms on their ability to extract salient patterns from a given peice of music. Your task will be to help us establish a baseline by annotating the phrases and repeated patterns in several hymn melodies and one improvised jazz solo.
